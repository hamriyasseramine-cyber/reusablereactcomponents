# Who it's for

This guide is aimed at people who are new to React, whether or not you already know some JavaScript. If you're comfortable with basic JS (variables, functions, arrays), you can move quickly through the fundamentals and focus on the React-specific sections. If you're a complete beginner, the JavaScript Fundamentals section will give you exactly what you need before moving on — no more, no less.

It's not meant to replace the official React documentation, but to give you a simpler, project-focused path to get productive faster.
